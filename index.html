FAQs

What types of shipping containers are available for sale on your portal??

We offer a wide range of shipping containers, including standard dry containers, refrigerated containers, open-top containers, and flat-rack containers.

What are the standard sizes of shipping containers, and which sizes do you offer?

The standard sizes of shipping containers are 20 feet and 40 feet. We offer both sizes, as well as other sizes, including 10 feet, 30 feet, and 45 feet.

What condition are the used shipping containers in, and do they come with any warranties?

Our used shipping containers are typically in good condition, with no structural damage or significant cosmetic defects. We offer different grades of containers to meet different budgets and requirements. We also provide warranties on our containers to ensure customer satisfaction.

Can you assist with the logistics of transporting the container to my location?

Yes, we have a network of transportation partners who can assist with the logistics of transporting the container to your location. We can provide you with a transportation quote based on your location and specific requirements.

How do I buy a shipping container through your portal, and what payment methods do you accept?

You can browse our inventory on our website and request a quote, and our sales team will provide you with the pricing and payment options. We accept various payment methods, including wire transfers, credit cards, and checks.

How long does it take to receive the container after purchase?

The delivery time depends on various factors, such as the distance between our facility and your location, the transportation mode, and any customs clearance procedures. However, we aim to deliver the container(s) as soon as possible and provide you with an estimated delivery time.

How long can I expect the container to last, and what maintenance is required?

Shipping containers are built to withstand harsh conditions, and with proper maintenance, they can last for many years. Maintenance involves regularly inspecting the container for rust, damage, and leaks, and treating any issues promptly. We can provide you with maintenance tips and guidelines to ensure the longevity of your container.

Do you offer any financing options for purchasing a shipping container?

Yes, we offer financing options for purchasing a shipping container, subject to credit approval. Our sales team can provide you with more information about our financing options and help you with the application process.
